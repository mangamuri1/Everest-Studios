Recording of Songs

State-of-the-art recording booths with premium microphones, preamps, and a pristine signal chain for vocals, instruments, and full orchestrations.

Re-Recording

Background score composition, dubbing sync, and complete re-recording sessions that breathe life into every cinematic frame.

Mixing & Mastering

04 🗣️

Dubbing

Film Editing

The Artist

Mahith
Narayan

Singer & Music Creator

Mahith Narayan is a celebrated singer, music artist, and the visionary founder of Everest Studios. With roots deep in the Telugu film industry, Mahith has lent his voice to some of the most memorable songs in contemporary Telugu cinema, blending raw emotion with technical mastery.

His journey from a passionate music student to a sought-after playback singer and studio owner reflects an unwavering dedication to sonic excellence. At Everest Studios, Mahith brings together his creative artistry and entrepreneurial spirit to create a world-class production environment for artists and filmmakers alike.
